Tony's Barbershop has been the go-to spot in Brooklyn since 2008. Started as a one-chair shop, we've grown into a community institution — where neighbors become friends and every client leaves looking their best.

Our barbers average 10+ years of experience. We believe a great haircut isn't just about the hair — it's about the conversation, the confidence, and the craft.
